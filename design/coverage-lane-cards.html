Coverage lanes — Business / Tech & Science / Global

The coverage channels render with the same broadsheet card as AU and Property, but with all the enrichment stripped out (sayThis, partnerAngles, counterpoint) — so the card promises "deep dive" and delivers an RSS headline. The current 3-up grid eats hero real estate per item and forces a lot of scrolling for very little payload.

The proposal — give coverage lanes their own visual language
  • Drop the serif headline + tall card for coverage lanes. Sans, smaller, ~⅓ the height per item.
  • Show 3–4× more items per viewport so a "scan" feels like scanning, not scrolling through hero spreads.
  • Keep amber/Playfair broadsheet language reserved for the enriched lanes (AU, Property) — so the visual hierarchy says: "this is the flagship; that is the scan."
  • Two variants below — pick the one that feels right.
